DOCUMENT: Vendor Agreement v3
PARTIES: Acme Corp ("Company") and Logistics Partners ("Vendor")
EFFECTIVE DATE: 2023-06-01
TERM: 24 months, auto-renewal unless 60-day notice given.

SCOPE OF SERVICES:
- Supply chain management and warehousing for all product lines.
- Freight forwarding and customs brokerage.

PRICING:
- Fixed monthly retainer: $45,000 USD
- Variable per-shipment: $120-$350 depending on weight class

TERMINATION:
- For convenience by either party with 60 days' written notice.
- For cause: Immediate termination for material breach.

IP RIGHTS:
- All proprietary logistics software developed under this agreement is jointly owned.
- Vendor retains rights to pre-existing IP.

INDEMNIFICATION:
- Vendor indemnifies Company against claims arising from Vendor's negligence.
- Cap on indemnification: 2x annual fees paid.

NON-COMPETE:
- Vendor agrees to a 12-month non-compete within the North American market.

CHANGE OF CONTROL:
- Agreement may be terminated by Company if Vendor undergoes a change of control event.
